Salad Recipe:

"Ingredients"

  1. Chopped raw vegetables
  2. Nuts and seeds
  3. Dried fruit
  4. Whole grains
  5. Beans and legumes
  6. Baked tortilla or pita chips
  7. olive oil

"procedure"

  1. Put all Ingredients in a mixing bowl.
  2. Put olive oil
  3. Sprinkle on salt and pepper
  4. Mix it well
